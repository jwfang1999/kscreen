Information

K-Screen is being developed to facilitate high throughput screening data management, visualization, analysis, and mining. The primary purpose of the software is for the HTS efforts at the University of Kansas, but we will create and make publicly available a generic version to share with the research community. The system consists of a web-browser based interface written in PHP, a MySQL database as its backend. Some of the scripts and database tables are based on moldb. The data processing and statistical analysis is performed using R scripts. It uses jpgraph or R to generate graphics. The overall design and features can be found in a recent poster.

K-Screen is currently developed and maintained by the Applied Bioinformatics Laboratory and the High Throughput Screening Laboratory at the University of Kansas. We invite other people interested in the project make contributions.

Current construction crew: David Tai, Rathnam Chaguturu, Jianwen Fang.

Contact: jwfang@ku.edu
